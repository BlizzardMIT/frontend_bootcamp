Student Experience Designer Role

The Next Generation Graduates Program

Students will participate in industry-led research projects and/or placements to build job-ready skills and provide short-term access to high-end talent for business in addition to completing a formal qualification relevant to AI.

You will have the opportunity to support the development and implementation of a suite of future critical capabilities.

Your duties will include Utilise data, trends and metrics to inform pedagogies, program delivery modification, ongoing evaluation of learning outcomes and ongoing support for students.

To be considered, you will need experience in course delivery and pedagogies (e.g., online and blended learning) in a higher education context.
